    Welcome to Zhengfei Zheng’s Homepage!


    I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, HKUST, Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Hai Yang. Prior to joining HKUST, I spent 3 wonderful years at Technion, Israel, obtaining my master degree in Transportation Engineering supervised by Prof. Jack Haddad and Prof. Shlomo Bekhor. I got my bachelor degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (Harbin Campus), China in 2013. My curriculum vitae here (Updated by Oct. 2022)

    Research Interests

    My primary research interests are in emerging problems related to smart mobility and data-driven transportation research, including:

    • Emerging Smart Mobility Service
      • Ride-sourcing markets
      • Pricing and regulations
      • Ride-pooling matching mechanism
      • Bike sharing
    • Artificial Intelligence for Transportation
      • Machine Learning for OD/speed/flow prediction
      • Reinforcement Learning applied to ride-sourcing service
    • Macroscopic Fundamental Diagram
    • Traffic Flow Theory and Signal Control
